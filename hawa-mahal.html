Hawa Mahal - Rajasthan

The Hawa Mahal, or "Palace of Winds", is an iconic palace located in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh, the palace's unique honeycomb design allowed royal ladies to observe street festivals while remaining unseen.

Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan

Best Time to Visit: October - March
