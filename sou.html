Statue of Unity - Gujarat

About:

The **Statue of Unity**, located in **Kevadia, Gujarat**, is the world's tallest statue at a height of **182 meters**. It is dedicated to **Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel**, the Iron Man of India, who played a key role in the country's unification after independence.

Unveiled in **2018**, the statue stands majestically on the banks of the **Narmada River**, surrounded by scenic hills and an integrated tourism complex.

Architectural Highlights:

  • World's tallest statue at **182 meters**.
  • Constructed using **reinforced concrete, bronze cladding, and structural steel**.
  • Viewing gallery at **135 meters**, offering panoramic views of the **Sardar Sarovar Dam** and the surrounding landscape.
  • Exhibits and museum detailing Sardar Patel’s life and India's unification process.

Things to Do:

  • Explore the **Statue of Unity Museum** and **audio-visual gallery**.
  • Visit the **Valley of Flowers**, spread across 600 acres.
  • Enjoy a boat ride on the **Narmada River**.
  • Take part in the laser light and sound show in the evening.

Location: Kevadia, Gujarat

Best Time to Visit: October - March (pleasant weather)

Visitor Tips:

  • Book your entry tickets online to avoid queues.
  • Visit early to get ample time for exploring the complex.
  • Carry a water bottle and comfortable walking shoes.
  • Don’t miss the **light and sound show** after sunset.
