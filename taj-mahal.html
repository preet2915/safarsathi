Taj Mahal - Uttar Pradesh

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is a symbol of eternal love and is famous for its stunning white marble architecture.

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Best Time to Visit: October - March
