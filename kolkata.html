Kolkata - West Bengal

About:

**Kolkata**, the **cultural capital of India**, is a vibrant city blending **colonial architecture**, **rich history**, and **modern lifestyle**. Known for its **art, literature, festivals**, and **delicious cuisine**, Kolkata offers visitors a unique blend of **tradition and progress**.

From the majestic **Howrah Bridge** to the iconic **Victoria Memorial**, every corner of the city tells a story. Kolkata is also famous for its **Durga Puja celebrations**, which have been recognized as a UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage.

Top Attractions:

  • **Victoria Memorial** - A grand white marble museum dedicated to Queen Victoria.
  • **Howrah Bridge** - One of the longest cantilever bridges in the world.
  • **Indian Museum** - The oldest and largest museum in India.
  • **Dakshineswar Kali Temple** - A sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Kali.
  • **Park Street** - A lively area known for **restaurants, nightlife, and colonial charm**.

Things to Do:

  • Take a **tram ride** through the old streets of Kolkata.
  • Explore the **Book Market at College Street** - a paradise for book lovers.
  • Enjoy authentic **Bengali cuisine** like **rosogolla, kosha mangsho, and fish curry**.
  • Experience the grand celebrations of **Durga Puja** if visiting in October.

Location: West Bengal, India

Best Time to Visit: October - February (Pleasant weather and festival season).

Visitor Tips:

  • Try using **hand-pulled rickshaws** for a traditional experience in North Kolkata.
  • Visit **Kumartuli** to see artists making **Durga idols**.
  • Enjoy a peaceful **boat ride on the Hooghly River** in the evening.
  • Explore both the **heritage side** and the **modern face** of the city.
