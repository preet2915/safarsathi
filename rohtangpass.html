Rohtang Pass - Himachal Pradesh

About:

**Rohtang Pass** is a high mountain pass located on the **Pir Panjal Range** of the **Himalayas**, about 51 km from **Manali**, Himachal Pradesh. At an altitude of **3,978 meters (13,050 feet)**, it serves as the gateway to **Lahaul and Spiti valleys**.

Rohtang Pass is famous for its **breathtaking views**, **snow-capped mountains**, **glaciers**, and **adventure sports**. It is one of the most visited places in Himachal Pradesh, attracting nature lovers, photographers, and thrill-seekers alike.

Top Attractions:

  • Snow-covered landscapes offering **skiing**, **snowboarding**, and **sledging** opportunities.
  • Stunning views of the **Chandra River** and surrounding peaks.
  • Gateway to **Lahaul and Spiti**, known for remote beauty.
  • Several Bollywood movies, including **Jab We Met** and **Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani**, were shot here.

Things to Do:

  • Enjoy **snow activities** like skiing and snow biking.
  • Capture stunning panoramic views of the Himalayas.
  • Visit nearby places like **Solang Valley** and **Beas Kund**.
  • Experience a thrilling drive on the **Manali-Leh Highway**.

Location: Near Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Best Time to Visit: May - October (Open season, weather permitting)

Visitor Tips:

  • Carry warm clothing even in summer, as the weather is unpredictable.
  • Rohtang Pass requires a **permit**, so apply in advance online.
  • Due to heavy snowfall, the pass is **closed in winter** (November to April).
  • Travel early in the day to avoid traffic congestion on the narrow roads.
