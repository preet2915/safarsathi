Pachmarhi - Madhya Pradesh

About:

**Pachmarhi**, often called the **"Queen of Satpura"**, is the only hill station in **Madhya Pradesh**. Known for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, caves, and lush forests, Pachmarhi is a popular retreat for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

It is part of the **Satpura Biosphere Reserve** and offers stunning views, ancient caves, and beautiful colonial-era buildings. Mythologically, it is believed that the Pandavas stayed here during their exile.

Top Attractions:

  • Bee Falls - A stunning waterfall and popular picnic spot.
  • Jata Shankar Cave - A sacred cave associated with Lord Shiva.
  • Dhoopgarh - The highest point in Madhya Pradesh offering mesmerizing sunsets.
  • Handi Khoh - A dramatic gorge with breathtaking views.
  • Pandava Caves - Ancient caves believed to have housed the Pandavas.

Things to Do:

  • Explore the serene forests and waterfalls.
  • Go trekking or nature walking in the Satpura ranges.
  • Experience breathtaking sunsets at **Dhoopgarh**.
  • Visit ancient temples and caves with mythological significance.

Location: Pachmarhi, Madhya Pradesh

Best Time to Visit: October - June (Pleasant weather)

Visitor Tips:

  • Carry comfortable walking shoes for exploring caves and waterfalls.
  • Book gypsy rides for Satpura National Park exploration.
  • Pack light woolens if visiting in winter.
  • Respect natural habitats and avoid littering.
