Kedarnath - Uttarakhand

About:

**Kedarnath**, located in the **Garhwal Himalayas** of **Uttarakhand**, is one of the most **sacred pilgrimage sites** for Hindus. Home to the ancient **Kedarnath Temple**, it is dedicated to **Lord Shiva** and is one of the **twelve Jyotirlingas**. The temple is situated at an altitude of **3,583 meters** and offers **awe-inspiring views** of snow-clad peaks and rugged landscapes.

Kedarnath is an essential stop in the **Char Dham Yatra**, attracting lakhs of devotees each year, despite its remote location and challenging trek.

Top Attractions:

  • **Kedarnath Temple** - One of the most revered Shiva temples in the world.
  • **Vasuki Tal** - A glacial lake offering serene views.
  • **Gandhi Sarovar** - A small crystal-clear lake near Kedarnath.
  • **Bhairavnath Temple** - Located on a hilltop near the main temple.

Things to Do:

  • Undertake the **Kedarnath Trek** (16 km from Gaurikund).
  • Witness the **majestic Kedarnath peak**, especially during sunrise.
  • Experience the **spiritual atmosphere** during morning and evening **Aarti** at the temple.
  • Explore **other nearby pilgrimage spots**, including **Triyuginarayan Temple**.

Location: Rudraprayag District, Uttarakhand

Best Time to Visit: May - June & September - October (The temple is closed during winters due to heavy snowfall).

Visitor Tips:

  • Carry **warm clothing**, even during summer, as temperatures drop significantly.
  • Prepare for a **difficult trek** or book a **helicopter service** for easier access.
  • Pre-book your **accommodation** during peak pilgrimage season.
  • Acclimatize yourself before trekking to avoid **altitude sickness**.
